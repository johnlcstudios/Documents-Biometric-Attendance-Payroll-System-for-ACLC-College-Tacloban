# About Us

## Face Recognition Based Attendance with Payroll Management System

**ACLC College Tacloban** – Streamlining attendance tracking and payroll processing through advanced face recognition technology.

Our system integrates biometric authentication with automated payroll computation, ensuring accuracy, efficiency, and security for the college’s administrative operations.
